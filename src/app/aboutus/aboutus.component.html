Who We Are

IKEA was founded in Sweden in 1943 by Ingvar Kamprad. Since then, we’ve grown from a small business selling household goods into one of the world’s most beloved furniture and home goods retailers.

Our vision is to create a better everyday life for the many people. We focus on offering affordable, well-designed, and functional home furnishings to customers all around the world.

Our Values

At IKEA, we believe in responsibility, sustainability, and community. We continuously work to minimize our environmental impact and improve the lives of people both inside and outside the company.

Our History

How IKEA became the world's most beloved furniture store.

1943 - The Beginning

IKEA was founded by Ingvar Kamprad in 1943 in Sweden. Initially, it was a small mail-order business selling small household items like pens and picture frames.

1956 - Flat-Packed Furniture

In 1956, IKEA revolutionized the furniture industry by introducing flat-packed furniture, making products more affordable and easier to ship. This innovation has defined IKEA’s identity ever since.

Present Day - Global Expansion

Today, IKEA has more than 400 stores in over 50 countries. From living rooms to kitchens, IKEA continues to offer affordable, stylish, and functional home furnishings to millions of customers worldwide.
